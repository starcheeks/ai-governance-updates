About This Newsletter

Our Mission

The AI Governance Newsletter exists to help compliance officers and auditors stay ahead of the rapidly evolving AI regulatory landscape without drowning in noise.

We believe busy practitioners deserve quick, actionable insights — not another wall of text. That's why we built a dashboard-first approach that answers your critical questions in under 5 minutes.

What Makes Us Different

📊 Dashboard-First Design

Five interactive cards give you the regulatory pulse at a glance: sentiment trends, key events, risk levels, upcoming deadlines, and historical context.

🎯 Practitioner-Focused

Written by compliance professionals for compliance professionals. Every update includes "What this means for you" sections that translate policy into business impact.

🌍 Global Coverage

Tracking AI governance across EU, US, UK, China, and emerging markets. We monitor federal, state, and international developments so you don't have to.

⚡ Actionable Intel

Color-coded severity levels, deadline countdowns, and risk assessments help you prioritize what matters most to your organization.

Publishing Schedule

Weekly on Fridays at 8:00 AM EST

We publish every Friday morning so you can review the week's developments and brief your leadership before the weekend. Breaking news updates are posted mid-week when critical developments occur.

How to Use This Newsletter

  1. Start with the dashboard — Get the regulatory pulse in under 5 minutes
  2. Check severity-coded events — Critical items (red) need immediate attention
  3. Review upcoming deadlines — Add urgent deadlines to your calendar
  4. Dive into relevant sections — Full details below the dashboard
  5. Share with leadership — Forward compliance callout boxes to stakeholders

💡 Pro Tip

Use the sentiment gauge as your north star. When it shifts into red/orange zones, your organization should prepare for stricter compliance requirements and higher scrutiny.
