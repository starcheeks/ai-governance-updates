## 🇪🇺 EU Enforcement Actions Begin

Ireland's Data Protection Commission issued preliminary findings against two companies for AI Act violations related to transparency requirements. Potential fines: €20M or 4% of global annual revenue.

**What to do:** Complete transparency documentation immediately and implement user notice systems for all AI applications.

## 🇺🇸 US Federal Procurement Guidelines

OMB clarified Executive Order 14110 requirements for federal agencies purchasing AI systems:

- Vendor certification process for AI safety and security
- Transparency documentation matching EU AI Act standards
- Impact assessments for high-risk applications
- Ongoing monitoring and incident reporting

**Timeline:** Certification process launches June 1, 2026.

## 📍 State-Level Advances

**California SB 1047:** Cleared Senate Appropriations; focuses on frontier model governance and safety standards.

**New York AI Employment Act:** Passed Assembly committee; regulates AI use in hiring and promotion decisions.

## 📊 Compliance Costs

Stanford HAI published research on EU AI Act compliance:
- Initial: €500K - €5M per organization
- Annual: 15-20% of initial investment

**Recommendation:** Budget 2-3x your initial estimate.
