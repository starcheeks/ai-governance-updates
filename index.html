European Union

Regulatory Activity

EU AI Act Annex 3 Schedule Updated with Stricter Compliance Timelines
The European Commission released revised implementation schedules for high-risk AI systems under Annex 3, moving several compliance deadlines forward by 3-6 months. Organizations must submit conformity assessments by April 15, 2026 instead of the previously announced July 1 deadline.
📰 European Commission 📅 February 28, 2026
Read more →
What This Means for You
If your organization develops or deploys high-risk AI systems in the EU, you now have 45 days to submit conformity documentation. Start internal audits immediately and engage with notified bodies for assessment scheduling.
EDPB Issues Guidance on AI and GDPR Intersection
The European Data Protection Board published detailed guidance clarifying how existing GDPR provisions apply to AI systems, particularly regarding automated decision-making and data minimization requirements.
📰 EDPB 📅 February 25, 2026

United States

Federal Developments

NIST AI Framework v2.0 Comment Period Closing Soon
The National Institute of Standards and Technology is accepting public comments on the proposed AI Risk Management Framework v2.0 through March 15, 2026. The updated framework includes expanded sections on generative AI and foundation model governance.
📰 NIST 📅 February 27, 2026
Read more →
What This Means for You
Review the draft framework and submit comments if your organization uses foundation models or generative AI. The final version will likely shape future federal procurement requirements and industry best practices.
